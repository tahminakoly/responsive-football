Every four years, people of Bangladesh engage themselves heavily in the FIFA World Cup though Bangladesh is light years away from it. This enthusiasm for the World Cup is evidence how much they love football. This is not a new-found passion though; rather a glorious past of football in Bangladesh drives the football-loving people to be engaged in the greatest show on earth. The passion is so high that they hoist the national flags of their teams on rooftops and engage in bitter arguments about their teams, like they once did during matches of country's two archrivals -- Abahani and Mohammedan. But those who once saw the best of Bangladesh football are now sorely watching the worst of it.
